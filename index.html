# Lemon is my LLM Research project

I've spent most of my time as of this writing (June 2026) criticizing or hating on LLM's (or rather AI of all types). I attended a meetup recently at 1rg on homecooked software. To my surprise I saw and hear more positive things about vibe coding than not. Up until this point I had dismissed the idea of LLM based coding as just nonsense deskilling, dependance building scam. To those concerns, I'm not completely overturned, I think they are still very valid concerns. So then the question is, how can we shape usage that doesn't cause these issues.

There are still the issues of extractive practices by players of big tech, environmental harm, straight up stealing and benefiting from other people's work and so much more. I don't know how to think about these things yet. I'm trying to read on this topic, talk to people about it, read other people's account on it and so on.
